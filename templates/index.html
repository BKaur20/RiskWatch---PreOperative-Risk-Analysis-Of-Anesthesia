About Us

RiskWatch is an invaluable tool that enhances the preoperative risk analysis of anesthesia. It serves as a valuable resource for healthcare professionals, aiding them in the assessment and evaluation of potential risks associated with administering anesthesia to patients prior to surgery.

It takes into account various factors such as the patient's age, medical history, current health status, etc. By inputting these variables, it generates patient's risk level based on different parameters, allowing anesthesiologists to make informed decisions and tailor anesthesia plans to individual patients. The analysis helps identify any potential complications, enabling the medical team to take necessary precautions and optimize patient safety during the perioperative period.
